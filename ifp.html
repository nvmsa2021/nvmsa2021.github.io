NVMSA’21 Instructions for Presenters

These instructions are for presenters at the 10th IEEE Non-Volatile Memory Systems and Applications Symposium.

Presentations: Authors are required to record videos of their presentations and prepare their slide decks prior to the conference event. Videos and slide decks are due Sunday, August 15, 2021 and must be submitted via easychair.

  • Authors must prepare a video of 20-25 minutes in length: This video will be played live for you by the NVMSA staff during the event at your designated talk time slot of the program and will be followed by a Q&A session that authors will answer live.
